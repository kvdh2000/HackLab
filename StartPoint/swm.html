Star Wars Misfits

While as the name suggests, Misfits is based on Star Wars source material, it's hardly what the story is about. The narrative of Misfits has always been found family, adventuring together, and growing stronger as people (both in roleplay, and out of roleplay as real people).

Now while the origin of this community server is based in drama and poor behavior, since me and some friends took control of it it's become a second online home for many. In it's hayday, Misfits saw literal thousands of messages a day, both in general chats, and in roleplays. In recent years, it's simmered down quite a lot, but the majority of the original members are still around, and new people come in from time to time. Roleplays are still going, building on a story 6 years in the making.
